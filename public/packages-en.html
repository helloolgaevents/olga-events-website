How to book

Two ways to begin, choose yours

With Olga Events everything starts with planning, the team that leads your wedding and is responsible for every moment. We add décor to planning, and here you have two comfortable paths: take both packages at once for the best price, or start with planning and add décor later. This is how we organise every project, to give you the best experience and allocate the right resources. Below we suggest which path is closer to you.

Recommended · best price

Planning + décor together

You take both packages at once and we run your wedding as one project from day one. Why it's the best choice:

1.Best price. Décor comes at a special, reduced rate, and your saving grows with the guest count.
2.Fast decision. In one step you close both planning and styling, cutting prep time by 60-70%. Especially valuable if the wedding is near.
3.Complete peace of mind. Everything booked at once, no more worrying about finding vendors in time or prices rising.
4.One vision, one team. Planning and décor are created together, one style, nothing lost between stages.
5.Price locked today. Décor at today's rate, no seasonal increase.

Booking payment: 50% of the total (a comfortable start without full prepayment).

The special décor price is our thank-you for trusting us with both planning and styling at once. That's why we meet you on price.

Ideal if the wedding is near, or you want to settle everything in one step, calmly and at the best price.

Planning only · flexible

Open budget

You start with the planning package, we add décor later. Why this path is convenient:

1.Calm start. You pay only for planning, no large sum up front.
2.Budget flexibility. We choose décor later, to your final budget.
3.Trust gradually. First you see our work in action, then you expand.
4.Your own pace. You spread decisions and payments over time, no rush.

Standard prices here, no discount: planning from AED 7,000, décor added later from AED 47,000 (grows with guest count).

The special discount applies to both packages only when booked together. Here you go calmer and more flexibly, but planning and décor are both at standard price.

Suits you if:
·there is time before the wedding and you want to start light;
·you've already planned décor, or plan it with another company, but need the professional support of a wedding planner and a coordination team.

Both paths lead to the same wedding of your dreams. If there is plenty of time and you'd like to go calmly, start with planning. If the wedding is near, or you want the best price, one style and to settle everything in one step, take both packages at once: it's both faster and better value.

The packages differ by level of styling, and so by price. Bloom, a neat base: compact centerpieces, a basic choice of flowers and candles. Signature, a step up: medium-sized arrangements, more flowers and candles, more expressive candleholders. Elegance, premium level: large, lavish arrangements, premium flowers and crystal candelabra. The higher the package, the richer the stage, backdrop and florals, hence the difference in price.

Each package is a complete set of everything needed for a beautiful wedding: stage, dance floor, backdrop, centerpieces with fresh flowers, venue styling with artificial flowers, VIP chairs, entrance styling, lighting. A convenient starting point with a clear price.

Additional elements stay optional, since every venue and every wedding is different. These can be:

  • staircase styling;
  • entrance-zone styling;
  • floor covering;
  • wall covering;
  • ceiling structures;
  • hanging installations;
  • photo zones;
  • custom-made structures.

Many couples simply don't need these elements. So we keep the package base simple and don't make you pay for what you may not use, letting every wedding grow to your style, priorities and budget. And if you want a fully bespoke approach, it's convenient to start with an open budget: take planning and fill the décor with exactly what you need.

Transparency · timing & preparation

How we work and why booking early matters

We want to be completely open with you, so you understand how our work is organised and can trust us with ease.

What's inside the packages is already ready. These elements are in our collection, with thought-through designs and clear prices. This standard set we assemble confidently and right on time, you don't need to wait.

Everything additional is created for your wedding. Floor and wall covering, canopies and ceiling structures, hanging installations, photo zones and stands, each such element we plan and cost separately: to your venue, its size, zones and theme.

Why additional décor takes time. To give you an exact price, we may need to:

  • visit the venue;
  • discuss the project with designers;
  • cost the flowers and materials;
  • align with the production team;
  • check our inventory;
  • order decorative elements from abroad;
  • custom-make pieces from acrylic, foam and glass;
  • bring in specialised contractors and factories;
  • run tests if needed.

Production itself often takes months.

We do have our own inventory, but it constantly needs:

  • painting;
  • restoration;
  • refinements;
  • adapting to each wedding.

Why 12 months is the ideal lead time. That much time lets:

  • the couple live and work calmly, without pressure;
  • the designers create beautiful concepts;
  • the production team prepare properly;
  • everyone make the best decisions.

And what if there's less time? Six months, three, sometimes even two, that's absolutely fine, and we take it on with joy 🙂 We simply adjust our speed and the whole process to your timeframe, and get everything done beautifully and on time.

Why we're still in love with "early": in our experience, when there's more time, couples truly enjoy the preparation, brides worry and stress less, and we have the time not only to plan the wedding but also to look after your mood and peace of mind, because that's part of our work too. But whatever the timeframe, a year or a couple of months, we're by your side from day one and carry everything through to the result.
